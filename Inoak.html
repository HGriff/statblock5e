Aura of Fire. (Molten)

At the start of each of Ino'ak's turns, each creature within a 5 foot radius takes 2d6 fire damage, and any flammable objects not being worn or carried catch fire. Any character that touchs Ino'ak witha melee attack also takes this damage.
